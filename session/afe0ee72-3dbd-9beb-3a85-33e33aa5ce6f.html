Yonatan Bergman

Senior Director of Engineering @ WeWork

A Whole New World: Taking your app global

Proposal

You've launched your first version and all your customers love it - been there, done that!

But wait, they're all from the US! How do you expand it around the world? There’s no one to tell you where to go.

In this talk I'm going to show you what I've learned from building the PayPal Mobile app and from growing WeWork's unbelievable sites to support more countries. I'm going to cover it all: from translations, i18n, money, dates, names and taxes.

So that you can get a new fantastic point of view and reach new customers in London, Beijing, Tel-Aviv and Paris.

About Reversim

Reversim (רברס עם פלטפורמה) is a Hebrew podcast by Ori Lahav and Ran Tavory which brings together software developers and product, with over 300 recorded episodes and a few thousands listeners.

The summit is our intention to create a conference for developers by developers. Like in the podcast, we bring you the content we are interested in, and we hope you will be too.
