The Singularity, demystified

Proposal

Different experts and futurists have different definitions to “the singularity”. They all however, focus on the point in time when computers and machines and their artificial intelligence will be so vastly advanced that they’ll take control over mankind. Consider “The Matrix” or SkyNet from “Terminator”.

If you think this is fantasy or at least something very far off into the future, you’ll be surprised to know that most experts expect this to become our reality in the next 30 years.

Confused? In this lightning talk I’ll explain what exactly is this singularity everyone is talking about, how AI comes into play and what it means for all of us.

If you’re a human being, this talk is for you.
